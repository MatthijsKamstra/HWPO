metcon

5 Sets of AMRAP 3...
20 Pistol
20 Box Jump Over @ 24/20"
Max Rep Bar Muscle-Up In Remaining Time

Rest 1:00 Between AMRAPs.

NOTES:
- If you do not have pistols, scale to 50ft dual dumbbell front rack walking lunges @ 2x50/2x35lbs, or a weight that you could complete unbroken.
- You should have around :60 for bar muscle-ups. If you have less time, scale the number of pistols or box jump overs.
- If you do not have bar muscle-ups, then scale to burpee pull-ups or jumping burpee pull-ups.
- For those of you looking for an extra challenge, do the box jump overs RX+ and use 30/24".
- For scoring purposes, include the pistols and the box jump overs in the total rep count for each AMRAP.
