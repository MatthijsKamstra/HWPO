metcon

10 Rounds For Time:
6 Burpee Get Overs *
30 Double Unders

Time Cap: 12 Minutes

NOTES:
* Burpee get-overs can be done on any implement, but jerk blocks are ideal. The get-over is a slower movement, but it still jacks your heart rate up. Aim to be between 40-48" for these.
- If you know that you will trip a lot on the double-unders, do single-unders instead, this should be a high heart rate workout!
