Back Squat
4x10 @ 60kg - 65kg // RPE 8
Rest 2-3 Minutes Between Sets.

NOTES:
- We will be focusing on higher-volume back squats for the next four-week block. These sessions will serve to build a strong base that we can build on later in the cycle. Time Cap: 15 Minutes

NOTES:
- This workout is a bit different than our typical metcon. Aim for a controlled pace on the ring dips and strict-handstand push-ups. Break both movements early and often to avoid failure.
